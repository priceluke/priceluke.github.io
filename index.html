Project Euler

April 15, 2019
Project Euler is a collection of computational problems that can be solved using various mathematical approaches in under a minute on an average CPU. There are nearly a million users on the site and over 600 problems to solve with new ones being added every few weeks [1]. These challenges have appealed to me from a problem-solving standpoint so from time to time I will complete a task. The solutions are available on my GitHub (don't tell) to showcase how I work through problems.
